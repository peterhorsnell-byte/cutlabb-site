Design limitations

What cuts clean - and what doesn't

Cut vinyl is a bold, high-contrast medium. It's not photo printing. A quick honesty check so your design lands perfectly the first time.

✅ Works beautifully

  • ✓ Bold logos, icons and monograms
  • ✓ Text, names, numbers and quotes
  • ✓ Clean outlines and silhouettes
  • ✓ High-contrast, solid-colour shapes
  • ✓ Simple layered / stacked colour artwork

⚠️ Usually won't cut

  • ✕ Detailed photographs or portraits
  • ✕ Gradients, shadows and soft fades
  • ✕ Tiny fine detail or thin hairlines
  • ✕ Full-colour printed image effects
  • ✕ Realistic textures and blends

Not sure which camp your idea falls into? Send it through anyway - we'll tell you honestly whether it can be cut, or suggest a cleaner version that will.
